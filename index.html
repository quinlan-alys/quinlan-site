About

Who is Quinlan?

My name is Quinlan.

I am an artist/photographer turned front-end web developer and UI designer. I am passionate about visual experience weather it be in the form of public art pieces or navagating through a website. Through all of my creative endeavours, I’m particularly interested in the audience and the unique way in which we all consume art and design differently.

I’m currently working on freelance projects, while on the hunt for a full-time role in front-end web development. Let's touch base!
